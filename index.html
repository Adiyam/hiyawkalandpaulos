We’ve met

How we met---To Our Parents

We met in the library late night studying for finals

How we met---To Everyone else

He--just returned to DC after finishing up his first semester of law school and out with his brother and his friends...She--out with her family and friend celebrating her cousin's Pre-wedding party."Pure" lounge was the venue that made it possible for us to meet...

First Date

After talking on the phone and texting for a few weeks we decided to meet for coffee January 8, 2009 at the Starbucks near Pentagon City mall. I asked my friend Betty to give me a call 30 minutes after I met up with him, my plan was to use her phone call as an excuse, just in case things weren’t going well. Needless to say the conversation was flowing and I never picked up Betty’s call, I ended up staying for 3 and ½ hour. Paulos walked me to Metro station....

Engagement

We talked about a traditional engagement; he was going to send Shemagli to my family to ask for my hand and then have an engagement party where he would propose in front of everyone we loved. The date was set for Shemagli to come and ask my parents however before that we went to Atlantic City for my birthday, the weekend was amazing on the last day Paulos gave me a birthday gift, one of the gifts was a collage he made with pictures and things he kept throughout the five years...
